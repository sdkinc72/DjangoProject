2021 BRONCO BADLANDS

aka BOONG-BOONG

The **2021 Ford Bronco Badlands** is an off-road-focused SUV designed for serious adventure enthusiasts. Equipped with a rugged 4x4 system, Bilstein shock absorbers, front and rear locking differentials, and a sway bar disconnect for maximum articulation, the Badlands trim offers outstanding capability on rough terrains. It features a powerful turbocharged engine, removable doors and roof, and a durable interior with marine-grade vinyl seating and rubberized flooring, making it ideal for both rugged expeditions and everyday driving. The Badlands trim also includes the GOAT (Goes Over Any Terrain) modes for optimal traction across different surfaces.
